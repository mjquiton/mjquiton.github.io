Cycle the Continental Divide

This annual 605 mile event winds through the beautiful rugged terrain of the Bridger Moutain Range crossing the Continental Divide several times! There are many check points along the way. See the mountains in all their glory while racing to the finish line!

September 18 - 23

Entrance Fee: $225/person

Entrance fee includes: Commemorative T-Shirt, commemorative water bottle, lodging, snacks and dinner each night of the race.

Race Route: Beginning in Bozeman, MT

Check point in Manhattan, MT

Check point in Lombard, MT

Check point in Townsend, MT

Check point in Helena, MT

Check point in Great Falls, MT

Check point in Lewiston, MT

Check point in Roundup, MT

Check point in Billings, MT

Finish in Bozeman, MT
